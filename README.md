🌿 CarbonTrace — Blockchain-Based Carbon Credit Verification System

CarbonTrace is a government-oriented digital platform designed to bring transparency, trust, and scalability to carbon credit generation and trading. The system integrates satellite-based vegetation analysis (NDVI), drone verification, and blockchain technology to scientifically measure environmental impact and convert it into verifiable carbon credits.

By leveraging IPFS for secure document storage and Ethereum blockchain for tamper-proof records, CarbonTrace ensures that every land registration, plantation activity, and carbon credit issuance is traceable, immutable, and trustworthy. The platform also enables a government-controlled marketplace, where companies can purchase verified carbon credits with full transparency.


⛓️ Blockchain Integration (Ethereum Sepolia)

CarbonTrace uses smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain to securely register land boundaries and transparently issue tokenized carbon credits.
Below are our core contracts deployed on the Ethereum Sepolia Testnet:

- 🌍 *Land Registry Contract:*  
  Registers land boundaries securely linked to IPFS.
